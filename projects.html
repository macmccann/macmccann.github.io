Planner@Brown
A four-year course planner for Brown University in development. Was originally conceived at Brown's Innovation Dojo.

Brown Computer Science Courses
A list of the courses in the Brown Computer Science department that can be filtered and sorted by pathway, course title, and more. Created with React.

Auxify
An unfinished project created over the summer of 2017. Auxify is a web application which would allow a user to host a session with a Spotify premium account where guests could connect and play songs on the host's media device. Created with Node.js.

Bears@Work
A project initiated by Fabrice Guyot-Sionnest that aims to keep Brown CS students in Rhode Island through financing internships at local tech companies, helping the companies reach out to Brown students, and creating a cohort of students who would form a community together.
